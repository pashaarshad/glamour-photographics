Our Legacy 40

40 Years.
Thousands of Stories.
One Legacy.

Founded in Bengaluru in 1982, Glamour Photographics introduced vibrant colour media to the city. Over four decades we have grown into a trusted name for weddings, corporate events, school photography, brand films, and long-form documentaries — serving global organisations and intimate families with equal devotion.

1982 Founded in Bengaluru. A new vision for colour photography and storytelling begins.
1995 Expansion into corporate photography. BIAL, Toyota, and GE among our earliest institutional clients.
2005 Cinematic wedding films introduced. A new language of love and celebration.
2015 Documentaries, brand films, and digital campaigns added to our repertoire.
2025 Four decades. Over 1,000 weddings. 500+ corporate events. The story continues.
